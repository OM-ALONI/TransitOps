How it works

Three steps from chaos to clarity.

01
Register assets

Add vehicles with registration numbers and load capacities. Onboard drivers with license details. Everything lives in one searchable list.

02
Dispatch a trip

Pick a vehicle and driver, enter cargo weight and destination. The platform validates capacity and availability, then marks both as “On Trip.”

03
Track & review

Log fuel, close maintenance tickets, complete trips. Dashboards and reports show fleet utilization, fuel efficiency, and operational cost in real time.

Vehicle lifecycle

Each vehicle moves through Available → On Trip → In Shop → Retired. Status updates happen automatically when you dispatch or maintain.

  • Unique registration enforced
  • Odometer tracking
  • Max load validation on every trip

Driver compliance

License expiry alerts, safety scores, and automatic status sync. Suspended drivers or expired licenses block trip assignment instantly.

  • Expiry date visibility
  • Safety score 0–100
  • Auto-flag for expired licenses

Operational reports

Fuel efficiency per vehicle, fleet utilization percentage, and ROI computed as (Revenue − Costs) ÷ Acquisition. Download CSV with one click.

  • Distance / Fuel metric
  • Cost breakdown (fuel + maintenance)
  • One-click CSV export
